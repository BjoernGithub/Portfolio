"What experience do you have?"

Over the years I have acquired a few experiences under my belt - and got room for many more to come!
With the regular 10-year danish school exam behind me, I finally graduated from the HF programme in the first half of 2021. After having chosen arts and mathematics as my higher focus for the final exams. With this, I felt that I already had a clear vision of what I wanted to do. Something creative! But alas, after getting my application for The Animation Workshop located in Viborg rejected, I found myself needing to find something else.
After being at a loss for a while, I came by the term "multimedia designer". And with a closer look I decided it was worth a try. Even if I didn't have the best knowledge for computers. And here I am now! With a completely new interest in coding and design after a few months as a multimedia designer at Zealand business academy.
And how can I forget my experiences with art? My journey hasn't been quite as professional as my short start with coding, but I've definitely gathered some knowledge over the years. My skill lies mostly on a tablet with drawing programs such as Procreate. But upon starting at Zealand, I've since added the whole Adobe package to my arsenal. It's still a lot of new things to learn, but Photoshop and Illustrator won't best me for long.
