Moohvie prototype

Ever been lost and unable to find out which movie to watch? Look no further than the Moohvie film generator.

During the last weeks of September 2021 me and a group of classmates were part of the entrepreneurship competition known as the Startup Mission. We were tasked to come up with a product from scratch and create it to whatever extend that we could. And after throwing around ideas of portable desks and mobile travel cards, we came to a much different idea.
A cow that generates 4 movies after being given a streaming service, a genre, year and rating.
Moohvie! Get it?
In this project I was tasked as the head of product and design. During this time I was solely working on a full prototype of how our app would look. Using Adobe XD, this was made nice and simple. And it was possible to link up the pages like an actual website.
And with my app prototype, me and my group were able to proceed and make a (somewhat) finished website.

Role: Design and product
Responsibility: Prototype in Adobe XD
